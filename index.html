Centralized and decentralized exchange Token baskets enable users to enter the exchange Token market with a single click, rather than search for liquidity of each Token. Users can obtain a balanced ratio of Exchange based Tokens with a single transaction.
Different Basket Themes such as “Decentralized supply chain” Tokens, “Decentralized media/entertainment” Tokens can easily be created and traded, hence enabling users new to crypto to easily purchase a balanced group of Tokens rather than search for liquidity of individual Tokens on multiple exchanges.
Every Basket created is a smart contract that is owned by the creator. With purchase of a Basket, ownership is transferred, hence it’s entirely peer to peer, and truly decentralized.
Create Debt based baskets using the Dharma protocol (coming soon).
Since the Baskets are basically smart contracts holding the underlying Tokens, liquidity of baskets is maintained entirely on the Blockchain.
