Learn How WandX Works for You

Multi-Blockchain DEX and Basket protocol

User experience of a centralized exchange on a DEX

With the WandX Desktop app, you can trade Ethereum or NEO based Tokens, create and manage your wallets, and get exposure to multiple Tokens with a single transaction using our Basket Protocol.

Trade on a DEX on multiple Blockchains

Trade on an Ethereum or a NEO based decentralized exchange on the WandX desktop app without the hassle of signing transactions with an external wallet.

Create and manage wallets

Signing transactions on a decentralized exchange requires users to load up a different wallet with the required Tokens. On the WandX Desktop app, you can do import an existing wallet or create your own, while always holding control of your private key. You can trade using any wallet on a Decentralized exchange on Ethereum or the NEO Blockchain.

Exposure to multiple Tokens with a single click

With the WandX Desktop app, you can buy multiple ERC20 or NEP5 Tokens with a single click, hence mimicking what is done in the real world with assets. This allows you to buy into multiple theme based Baskets, hence enabling you to gain exposure to a group of Tokens and hence balance out any potential risk.

Centralized and decentralized exchange Token baskets enable users to enter the exchange Token market with a single click, rather than search for liquidity of each Token. Users can obtain a balanced ratio of Exchange based Tokens with a single transaction.

Different Basket Themes such as “Decentralized supply chain” Tokens, “Decentralized media/entertainment” Tokens can easily be created and traded, hence enabling users new to crypto to easily purchase a balanced group of Tokens rather than search for liquidity of individual Tokens on multiple exchanges.

Every Basket created is a smart contract that is owned by the creator. With purchase of a Basket, ownership is transferred, hence it’s entirely peer to peer, and truly decentralized.

Since the Baskets are basically smart contracts holding the underlying Tokens, liquidity of baskets is maintained entirely on the Blockchain.
